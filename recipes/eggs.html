Eggs Recipe

Description

These eggs are an old recipe past down to me from my Mexican Grandmother.

Ingrediants
  • Two Eggs
  • Corn Oil
Steps
  1. Crack eggs
  2. Break apart and mix in bowl
  3. Heat pan
  4. Add oil to pan
  5. When the pan is hot enough, add eggs
  6. Move around while cooking until they're nice and fluffy
